Liverpool FC

The History of the Club

Founded in 1892

Liverpool Football Club was founded in 1892 after a dispute with Everton FC. The club was formed by John Houlding and began playing at Anfield.

Early Success

The club won its first league title in 1901 and quickly became one of the top teams in England.

The Shankly Era

Bill Shankly transformed Liverpool in the 1960s, leading them to multiple league titles and building a legacy.

European Glory

Liverpool has won several European Cups, including the famous 2005 comeback in Istanbul.

Modern Era

Under Jürgen Klopp, Liverpool won the Champions League in 2019 and the Premier League in 2020.
